About LLI Strategies

LLI Strategies brings together expertise in historical research, cultural resources, data analysis, and strategic planning to serve public agencies, nonprofit organizations, educational institutions, and private developers. Our work bridges past and present, using historical insights and rigorous analysis to inform contemporary challenges in education, preservation, urban planning, and community development.

With advanced training in American Studies, history, and quantitative methods, combined with practical experience in strategic planning and community engagement, we deliver research and consulting services that are both intellectually rigorous and immediately actionable.
